    • Mobile Top-Up

      How do I purchase a Top-Up on iMARKET Site?

      Click on the Top-Up Banner from the Home Page or this link here

      You are encouraged to Log-In or Sign-Up with us so you can track your orders for future reference

      Key in your Mobile number and press enter

      Choose the product type – for voice, loads and bundles please click on Voice. For Data packs, click on Data

      Do remember to check your number before paying. Have you entered the correct Number?

      Once you have paid, we will process your order. Do not forget to take note of the Mobile Number you entered and iMARKET Order number, in case you need to contact us for any queries related to this order.

      I topped-up the wrong number, can I cancel and get a refund?

      Unfortunately, once a Top-Up number has been processed and delivered by the Telco, it’s not possible for the Telco/Provider to reverse the Top-Up. We also show you the number and telco that you are about to pay before you make the actual payment. Refer our T&C's for further information.

      I topped-up the wrong amount, can I cancel and get a refund?

      Unfortunately, once a Top-Up number has been processed and delivered by the Telco, it’s not possible for the Telco/Provider to reverse the Top-Up. We also show you the amount that you are about to pay before you make the actual payment. Refer our T&C's for further information.

      Can I cancel / replace my Top-Up?

      Unfortunately, once a Top-Up number has been processed and delivered by the Telco, it’s not possible for the Telco/Provider to reverse the Top-Up. Refer our T&C's for further information.

      What happens if my Top-Up is not processed?

      In case of a failure from the Telco or Service Provider's end, you will receive a Failure Notification on the iMARKET Screen along with our Order No for your reference. A refund processes will also be initiated automatically based on your payment method. If you had paid via Bank Transfer, ATM or any of the OTC / Pay Later methods, please call us in order for us to process your refund as we will need additional details.

      The iMARKET screen shows a Success but I did not receive anything on my phone.

      We are sorry to hear that. Here are few things you could do:

      1. Check if you are in an area of good connectivity

      2. Did you enter the correct number?

      3. If you were logged in, check your Order History and see what number you had entered.

      4. Reach out to our Customer service and we will be happy to assist you.
