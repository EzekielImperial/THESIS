    • Product Information

      Where can I get more product information?

      On the product page, you can read all the product information (e.g. product size, warranty duration, manufacturer name, etc.) under the tab "Product details" and "Specifications".

      Where do the products of iMARKET come from?

      iMARKET has a vast array of Products which has 3 different ways from where it was sourced:

      Retail – items that are sold and fulfilled by iMARKET

      Marketplace – items that are sold by merchants

      Crossborder – items that are shipped from overseas

      What are Retail Products and how can I identify them?

      Retail products are items that are sourced and sold by iMARKET itself. This can be identified on the product page on the items that are labeled as "Sold and Fulfilled by iMARKET".

      What are Marketplace Products and how can I identify them?

      iMARKET Marketplace products are items being sourced and sold by different Merchants who use the iMARKET website as a platform where they can showcase unique products at very affordable price. These type of merchandise can be identified on the Product page as "Sold by (Merchant Name), Fulfilled by iMARKET".

      Why are iMARKET products cheaper than others?

      We have a large warehouse that can accommodate many products in stock. Moreover, we can also buy goods directly from manufacturers with a cheaper price.

      How do I know the stock iMARKET have?

      Choose the product you want and go to its product page.

      You will see the stock information on the right side of the page, where it shows clear number of stock if below 5 or mentions "In Stock" if the available stock is more than 5.
