      How can I cancel my order?

      Cancelling an order is fast and easy with our online cancellation form.

      To use the form, you need to place the order with your iMARKET account (signup here if you don’t have an account) or if you used guest check out, please sign up using the email address used at the time the order is placed.

      1. Login to your account

      2. Go to "My orders" screen and select “Cancel” on the order you wish to cancel.

      3. Fill in the form:

      - Select the item you wish to cancel

      - Select your cancellation reason

      - Make sure you have read and accepted iMARKET’s cancellation policy

      - Click on "NEXT"

      Your order status will be updated to “Canceled” within 60 minutes; please wait for the system to refresh or wait for an email notification confirming your cancellation request.

      NOTE :

      Cancellation can only be accepted during the packaging of your item. You will not be able to access the online cancellation form after your order has been packed or after 6 hours after placing the order for Cross-Border items (Imported).

      Should you have any issues/concerns, please feel free to contact us via www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact.

      What is iMARKET’s Cancellation policy?

      Before cancelling an order, please read and understand the following terms & conditions:

      1. Cancelled order cannot be recovered on our system.

      2. Any vouchers, discounts, or promotions applied in the order cannot be reused.

      3. Price is subject to change depending on iMARKET promotion programs. We do not commit to offering the same price if you re-order.

      4. In case of prepayment via credit/debit cards, your money will be refunded automatically to your account within one to two billing cycles (one billing cycle is 1 month); depending on your bank policy.

      5. If a store credit has been used in the order, please contact our 24/7 Customer Care to request for a new voucher code.

      6. The remaining order’s value after you canceled one or several items has to fulfill the same conditions for discount, promotions, or minimum basket fee exemption applied to the order. If not, your order may be canceled.

      Can I cancel an item or several items in my order?

      Yes, you can cancel an item or several items in your order under the following conditions:

      The order was placed within 6 hours before the form is submitted for Cross-border item (imported).

      The item(s) you want to cancel is not yet shipped for local items.

      The remaining value after cancellation should still meet the conditions of discount, promotions and/or minimum basket fee.

      Do I have to pay any extra cost for using online cancellation form?

      Online cancellation form is free of charge.

      However, if after you canceled one or several items in your order, the remaining order's value does not match our conditions for discount, promotions or free shipping charge, iMARKET will cancel the whole order. You can re-order the item(s) you want again.

      How do I know if my cancellation request was accepted?

      After submitting the online cancellation form, you will see the line "Your cancellation request was successfully submitted" on the website indicating you have successfully sent your cancellation request to us. iMARKET will process your request within 60 minutes upon receiving the request.

      To know whether or not your order was canceled, you can log into your iMARKET's account and check "My Orders" page. If your item or order status is “Canceled”, you can rest assured that it was canceled successfully.

      In case your cancellation request is not processed by iMARKET after 60 minutes, your order may have already been shipped out and cannot be canceled. Please contact us via www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact for further assistance.

      I just canceled my order. When will I get my money back?

      In case of cancellations, your refund will be processed within two business days from submission of the online cancellation form.

      We will update you via email once your refund has been initiated. The total time for the refund to be reflected in your account depends on your refund method and issuing bank.

      Click here for more details on refunds.

      Can I cancel the order and reorder using a voucher?

      Yes, you may cancel your order and re-order using a voucher provided that the order has not been endorsed or packed by iMARKET or our Partner merchant.

      How can I change delivery address/ phone number/ recipient for my existing order?

      If you want to change:

      Delivery address for your existing order

      Phone number for your existing order

      Recipient for your existing order

      Please send us your inquiry at www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact. We will contact you as soon as possible.

      NOTE: Delivery addresses, phone numbers and recipient names can only be changed if the orders have not been endorsed or packed by iMARKET or our Partner merchant. Once shipped, we can no longer change the invoices since these are already printed and orders have already been handed over to our third-party couriers.

      If you want to change your account information or future order information, you can log in to your iMARKET account and follow the steps here.

      Can I change my payment method after order has been made?

      At iMARKET, we currently do not have an option to change the payment method applied during checkout. Hence, we suggest you place a new order and cancel your existing order immediately by sending us a request at www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact or by logging in to your account and follow the steps provided above..

      Can I change items from my order after order has been processed?

      We currently do not have an option to change nor add an item once the order has been placed. You may request for cancellation so you can place a new order instead.

      I accidentally ordered same items. Can I cancel one?

      We can cancel one item (in one order number) if you accidently ordered it twice or if same item is ordered under a different order number, you could easily cancel your order by sending a request at www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact or by logging in to your account and follow the steps provided above.

      I received an incomplete order, what should I do?

      Please notify us immediately at www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact so we can assist you promptly.

      If what missing from your order is a gift/ bonus, we will push to deliver it to you soon.

      If what missing from your order is a part of a product or an item, we will need you to send the whole order back to us. In this case, to assure prompt return resolution, please pack the item in its original package with full accessories and buying invoice.

      Kindly read and follow our How to Return guide here.

      I received the wrong item, what should I do?

      If the item you received is not what you originally ordered, we will need you to send the whole order back to us. We are happy to deliver the correct item for you.

      Returning an item is absolutely free. Kindly read and follow our How to Return guide here.

      I received damaged/ defective item, what should I do?

      If the item you received is damaged/ defective, we will need you to send the whole order back to us. Please notify us immediately at www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact. We will contact you right away.

      To assure prompt return resolution, please pack the item in its original package with full accessories and sales invoice, then bring the item to any LBC branch.

      Kindly read and follow our How to Return guide here.
