    • Place Your Order

      How do I place an order?

      For full instruction, click here http://www.iMARKET.com.ph/how-to-buy/

      I have ordered and confirmed payment with iMARKET. What should I do next?

      Once your order has been verified, we'll take care of everything for you. All you have to do is wait for your package to be delivered.

      How can I order without an account on iMARKET website?

      You can absolutely order on iMARKET without an account:

      1. Choose the product you want and proceed to checkout

      2. At Email Login step, enter your email address and choose "Continue without password"

      3. Enter your delivery information and choose your payment method

      4. Place your order

      For a detailed instruction on how to order, check out www.iMARKET.com.ph/how-to-buy

      We encourage you to create an account to get updated with iMARKET's latest and attractive promotions, plus to have your information and order history saved for your best online shopping experience.

      NOTE: The next time you try to place an order using the same email address, you will be prompted that an account already exists under your email address. This is for the account that was automatically generated for you when you first placed an order as a guest. In this way, you have your information and order history saved for your best online shopping experience.

      To access your account, click on the "Lost your password" link and an email will be sent to you containing your password. Log-in at www.iMARKET.com.ph using this password and place your order.

      Can I call iMARKET to place an order?

      We can assist you to place your order over the phone if your preferred payment method is Cash On Delivery (COD).

      However, we highly recommend you to check out how to make a purchase online here www.iMARKET.com.ph/how-to-buy.

      If in any situation you cannot place an order online, please contact our Customer Service [click], we will be more than happy to assist you.

      How to remove an item in your shopping cart?

      To remove an item in your shopping cart, you can:

      Click on the Cart icon on top of iMARKET page to go to your shopping cart

      Click on the "X" icon

      Your cart will be updated and that item will be removed.

      Will I receive any order confirmation after placing an order?

      An order confirmation with order summary will be sent to you through email and SMS right after you have placed your order.

      We will also send email and SMS notifications to keep you updated frequently on your order status.

      Can I place a corporate/bulk order?

      We no longer cater corporate sales/ bulk orders.

      Yet you may contact us at www.iMARKET.com.ph/contact for assistance on placing an order of multiple items.
